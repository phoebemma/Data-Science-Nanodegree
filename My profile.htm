My journey into Data Science

My current status

I cannot remember exactly when my interest in data science started, but I remember I started my blog The Informed Mum in 2015 shortly after I had my first child. It was originally meant to address some of the misconceptions and myths related to motherhood and childcare in my part of Nigeria. It slowly metamorphosed into a maternal and child health advocacy blog. During content development, I learnt HTML and some CSS.

In 2017, I started Python courses, first at CodeAcademy and later at Udacity where I moved beyond Python to R. I have since been a part of almost every free coding bootcamp I came across. I have also gained earned a couple of programming/machine learning scholarships.

At present, I am enrolled in a PhD program where I am working towards building and optimising machine learning models. This html script is a way for me to dust up my html and css skills and probably add some Javascript as well. From my mainly amateaur point of view, data science needs a good dose of

  • Interest in data science
  • Curiosity
  • Patience
  • Humility and the ability to accept that your codes can be horrible
  • And for female data scientists; confidence in yourself as you will meet people who think Data Science is too tough for persons with XX chromosomes
